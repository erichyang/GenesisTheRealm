Apply today!

Looking to join our family? Check out our manual, featuring great hits such as: The Application Link, Realm Rules Defined, and The Nature of Genesis! You'll be able to learn about some of the ins and outs of our world, and read about the entirety of the application process.

We use Discord, which is a group-chatting platform for gamers. It's how we communicate outside of the realm, make important announcements, host events such as Hunger Games Giveaways and the Genesis Building Contest. It's completely free, and required to be a part of Genesis. We wouldn't be where we are today without a way to communicate outside of the world.

Our ultimate goal is for the realm to not just be about the gameplay, but about the people. With that in mind, our application process is designed to be stricter than most. Thanks to this process, we can pride ourselves on providing a safe and enjoyable experience for our players. We promise it'll be worth the wait, and hope to see you exploring Genesis soon!
