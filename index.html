About Us

Genesis is a survival bedrock realm founded in June of 2016. Our realm features pure vanilla gameplay on hard difficulty, with achievements enabled!

We're a tight-knit community of players from all over the world, each with their own play style, interests, and strengths. We value community and collaboration, creativity, and respect. Genesis has had setbacks and challenges over the years, but we meet those challenges head-on and continue to evolve and improve to the best of our ability. We're fun, goofy, and a family-like group who really enjoy playing Minecraft together!

THE GENESIS CULTURE

Genesis has always been a community-first realm. Our group thrives off of collaboration and unity. Members are often eager to set up community/public locations to be enjoyed by the entire group, as well as share resources with one another!

Spawn

Every season, all members are able to vote for their desired spawn biome! This is where we are meant to stay together for the first few weeks of a new season and build our little starter homes.

Picture album

The Shopping Region

While we do love sharing things with one another, we still value and encourage our economy to thrive! A designated shopping area is built near spawn every season where members may build up their own shops for resources or services they wish to sell.

Picture album

The Nether Hub

To make traveling more convenient, there is a nether hub set up every season with 4 main tunnels that follow the cardinal directions. Members use these main tunnels to connect up to their main bases and other locations!

The Common Grounds

The common grounds is the dedicated area for a majority of our community/public farms and projects to be built on, as well as the Community Pantry, our storage for items meant to be used for any community projects!

The Arcade

The dedicated location for us to set up some games and fun rides the group can enjoy when on the realm together!
